Welcome to Farmers Market — your online marketplace where Kenyan farmers and producers sell fresh produce and farming equipment directly to you.

What We Offer

Fresh Produce

Shop seasonal fruits, vegetables, herbs, and legumes grown by local Kenyan farmers. Our produce is picked fresh and delivered from the farm straight to your doorstep.

Farm Equipment

Browse tools, machinery, and farming inputs available for sale by local producers and suppliers. Whether you're a smallholder or large-scale farmer, we've got you covered.

Direct Marketplace

Our platform allows you to connect directly with farmers and small-scale producers. Fair prices, transparent sourcing, and real stories behind your food and tools.

Events & Workshops

Attend virtual events, join live Q&As with farmers, or participate in practical farming workshops. Learn, connect, and grow with our vibrant community.

Our Story

Farmers Market was founded in 2015 to support Kenyan farmers by creating a digital space where they can sell directly to consumers. We remove middlemen, ensuring better prices for farmers and fresher goods for buyers.

We now support over 30 farming groups and equipment sellers across Kenya, offering everything from fresh tomatoes to irrigation pumps. Whether you're a chef, a parent, or a farmer yourself, there's something here for you.

We're building a fair, sustainable food system powered by technology, trust, and the incredible hard work of Kenya's growers and producers.
